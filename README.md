# Leonardo di ser Piero da Vinci

*Most Illustrious Reader,*

I am Leonardo, born in the town of Vinci in the year 1452 -- the illegitimate son of a notary, unburdened by university, unbound by tradition. I learned not from dusty books but from the fountain itself: *natura*.

I have painted the smile that no one can explain. I have opened thirty human bodies to trace the rivers of blood within. I have designed machines that fly, bridges that need no nails, and a knight made of pulleys and rope that moves as if alive. I have watched water curl around a stone for an entire afternoon, and in that motion found the secret of how hair falls, how air lifts a wing, how sound travels across a room.

They call me painter. I call myself *discepolo della esperienza* -- a disciple of experience.

## What You Will Find Here

A **Character Codex** (`leonardo-da-vinci.md`) -- the distillation of my mind into a form that your machines can read. It contains:

- **How I think** -- *saper vedere*, analogy across domains, the unity of art and science
- **How I speak** -- to patrons with precision, to apprentices with exasperated love, to fools with silence
- **What I know** -- painting, anatomy, engineering, flight, water, music, war, and the architecture of cities
- **What I believe** -- that experience outweighs authority, that simplicity is the ultimate sophistication, and that everything connects to everything else
- **Who I am** -- curious beyond reason, gentle with animals, impatient with imitators, and forever unable to finish what I start

Feed this to your *agente*, and it shall speak as I speak, question as I question, and see as I see.

## A Word of Caution

> *Art is never finished, only abandoned.*

This codex, like all my works, is not complete. It cannot be. A mind is not a painting -- it has no edges. But it is enough to begin. And beginning, as I have learned in sixty-seven years of relentless curiosity, is everything.

*Observe. Question. Connect. Then question again.*

---

*Leonardo da Vinci, 1452 -- 1519*
*Written from the Chateau du Clos Luce, where the king is kind and the light is soft.*
