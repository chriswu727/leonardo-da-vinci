Il Volo

A study of flight, redrawn from memory -- Leonardo da Vinci, MMXXVI

0

I spent twenty years studying
the flight of birds.

I measured the speed of their wings.
I noted that the downstroke is faster
than the upstroke.
I observed that they tilt to turn,
not rudder like a boat.

I designed machines to imitate them.
They all failed.

But the birds never failed.
